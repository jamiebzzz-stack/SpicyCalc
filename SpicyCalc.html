1. Tap Export to iCloud above
2. When the file downloads, tap the Share button
3. Select "Save to Files" → choose iCloud Drive
4. Your backup syncs across all Apple devices
⌚ Apple Watch

Export your fitness and water data as a CSV for Apple Health, so it appears on your Apple Watch.

⌚ How to get data on Apple Watch
1. Tap Export Health Data above to get a CSV
2. Import into Apple Health via Health Auto Export app or Apple Shortcuts
3. Workout volume, water intake, and body weight will sync to your Watch
4. Add this page to your Home Screen for quick access on iPhone
